About Underwater Basket Weaving

Underwater Basket Weaving has been seen as a joke life skill for several years. Our mission was to take this idea of a useless skill and show people just how enriching it can actually be.

Our company started on a rainy day, when I and several friends wanted to weave baskets outside. Instead of letting such sky water prevent us from weaving our baskets, we decided to start a business.

So some friends and I took it one step further and formulated our company dedicated to teaching those the art of weaving baskets in water. If it's anything the rain taught us, is to never let water ruin your dreams!
